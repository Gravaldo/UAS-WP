Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how TravelMe collects, uses, and discloses your personal information.

1. Information Collection

We collect information that you provide to us directly, such as when you create an account, update your profile, or communicate with us. This includes:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, phone number, etc.
  • Payment Information: Credit card details, billing address, etc.
  • Travel Preferences: Destination preferences, travel history, etc.

2. Use of Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • To provide and maintain our services: Ensure smooth booking and travel experiences.
  • To improve our services: Analyze usage and improve features.
  • To communicate with you: Send updates, promotions, and support information.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties without your consent, except as necessary to:

  • Comply with legal obligations.
  • Protect and defend our rights and property.
  • Prevent or investigate possible wrongdoing.

4. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access. This includes:

  • Encryption: Secure your data during transmission.
  • Access Controls: Limit access to personal information to authorized personnel only.

5. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website.
